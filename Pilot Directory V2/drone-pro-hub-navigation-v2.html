Navigation & Flow Recommendations

Navigation

Separate "Find" from "Learn"

Producers (clients) want to find pilots. Pilots want to learn and grow. Don't mix these in one menu ‚Äî it confuses both audiences.

Conversion

Sticky CTA on Every Page

"List Your Services" should follow users as they scroll. It's your primary conversion action for growing the directory.

Trust

Show Credibility Early

Your Hollywood credits are your biggest differentiator. "From the team behind Mission: Impossible" should be visible within 2 scrolls on any page.

SEO

Location Pages Are Critical

Auto-generate /pilots/australia/sydney pages for every city with 3+ pilots. These will rank for "drone pilot [city]" searches.

Email

Capture Emails Everywhere

Every content page should have a lead magnet. Producer Guide ‚Üí "Download PDF". Gear Review ‚Üí "Get Discount Code". Course Page ‚Üí "Free Mini Course".

Mobile

Bottom Nav on Mobile

For logged-in users on mobile, use a bottom navigation bar (like Instagram). Thumb-friendly, always accessible, modern.

Speed Records

Integrate, Don't Isolate

Speed Registry adds massive credibility. Feature "Current World Record: 661 km/h" prominently on the homepage. Link to it from pilot profiles.

Content

YouTube Embeds > Links

Don't just link to YouTube. Embed videos directly on guide pages and reviews. Keeps users on-site and improves time-on-page for SEO.
