Understanding Costs

What Affects Pricing

Location & Travel

Remote locations may require travel fees, accommodation costs, or travel days. International work typically includes travel costs and may have higher daily rates.

Duration & Setup

Half-day rates are typically 60-70% of day rate. Multi-day shoots often get discounts. Complex setups or aerial surveys may command premium rates.

Experience & Specialization

Oscar-winning cinematographers, specialized FPV pilots, or world-record holders command premium rates. Your portfolio and credits matter.

Equipment & Crew

Cinema cameras cost more than standard payload. Additional crew (assistant pilots, spotters, safety personnel) adds to the total cost.

Permit & Insurance

Complex airspace requires special permits and increased insurance. Restricted areas or high-value productions need additional coverage.

Post-Production Rights

Exclusive footage rights, archive rights, or commercial licensing adds to the cost. Usage rights vary by agreement and production type.
