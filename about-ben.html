Ben Biggs has achieved what many in the drone industry thought impossible. As the holder of the world record for fastest speed drone at 661 km/h, Ben represents the cutting edge of drone engineering and pilot skill.

But the world record is just one part of Ben's story. His expertise in high-performance drone builds, custom engineering, and speed optimization has made him an invaluable advisor to Drone Pro Hub. Ben combines technical precision with practical experience gained from thousands of hours pushing drone technology to its limits.
