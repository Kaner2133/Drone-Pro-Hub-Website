About Drone Pro Hub

Connecting the world's best drone pilots with the productions that need them. Founded by industry veterans with credits on Hollywood's biggest blockbusters.

Our Founder

Aidan Kelly's Story

Aidan Kelly's journey in drone cinematography began on some of Hollywood's biggest productions. As co-founder of XM2 Group and founder of Drone Pro Hub, Aidan has shaped the future of aerial cinematography in film and television.

With credits on Mission Impossible, John Wick, and countless other blockbusters, Aidan understood a critical gap in the industry: pilots were scattered, difficult to find, and producers had no reliable way to connect with specialized talent.

Drone Pro Hub was born from the need to unite the global drone community and make professional aerial cinematography accessible to productions worldwide. Today, we've connected over 500 verified pilots with productions across 85+ countries.
