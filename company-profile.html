About XM2 Group

XM2 Group is a world-class heavy lift drone cinematography company based in Sydney, Australia. Founded in 2015, we've established ourselves as the go-to specialists for premium aerial cinematography on high-budget productions, including multiple Hollywood blockbusters.

With over 18 years of combined aerial experience and 12 heavy-lift aircraft, we deliver broadcast-quality imagery from the sky. Our team understands production workflows and delivers on tight timelines with professional crews.

From feature films to commercials, we've worked with major studios and production companies worldwide, pioneering new approaches to heavy-lift cinematography and motion control systems.
