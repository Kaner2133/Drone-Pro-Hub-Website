Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Drone Pro Hub

How do I get listed as a pilot?

Simply sign up and create your profile. Verify your credentials, insurance, and licenses. Your profile will be live within 24 hours.

Is there a cost to list my profile?

No, listing your profile is completely free. You only pay when you complete a paid job through the platform.

How do I find pilots for my production?

Use our search feature to filter by location, platform, and specialty. Post a job brief and receive quotes from qualified pilots worldwide.

What does verification include?

We verify pilot licenses, insurance coverage, and production history. Every listed pilot is checked against industry standards.

Can I access the community if I'm not a pilot?

Yes, producers and industry professionals are welcome to join. Different membership tiers offer different features.

How do partnerships work?

We offer affiliate, sponsorship, and advertiser partnerships. Contact our partnerships team to explore opportunities.
