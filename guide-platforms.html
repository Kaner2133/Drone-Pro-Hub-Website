DJI Mavic Series

Portable Professional Power

The Mavic series represents the sweet spot of professional drone cinematography: excellent image quality in an incredibly portable package. The Mavic 3 Pro and Mavic 3 Cine deliver Hasselblad-quality footage with a 4/3" sensor, ProRes recording options, and flight times exceeding 40 minutes. It's the go-to choice when you need professional results without the complexity of larger platforms.

Sensor Size
4/3" CMOS
Max Resolution
5.1K / 50fps
Flight Time
43 minutes
Weight
895g

Signature Shot Types

Establishing Aerials

Wide shots that set location context. Perfect for opening sequences and scene transitions.

Reveals & Orbits

Smooth circular movements around subjects. Great for property tours and product reveals.

Top-Down / Nadir

Looking straight down for geometric patterns, crowds, and unique perspectives.

Real Estate Marketing Corporate Videos Wedding Films Travel Content Social Media Events News/Journalism YouTube Productions

DJI Inspire 3

The Broadcast Industry Standard

The Inspire 3 is the workhorse of professional aerial cinematography. With a full-frame sensor, interchangeable lenses, and 8K ProRes RAW recording, it delivers broadcast and cinema-quality footage that matches or exceeds traditional camera packages. The dual-operator capability allows separate control of flight and camera, enabling complex, precise movements that single-operator systems can't achieve.

Full Frame
Max Resolution

Complex Camera Moves

Dual-op allows pan/tilt independent of flight path. Shoot reveals while flying sideways.

Tracking Shots

Follow moving vehicles or talent with professional smoothness and precise framing.

Cinematic Aerials

Shallow depth of field, anamorphic looks, and color science that matches cinema cameras.

Heavy Lift Platforms

Cinema Cameras in the Sky

Heavy lift drones like the Freefly Alta X, DJI Matrice 600, or custom builds can carry full cinema cameras including RED Komodo, ARRI Alexa Mini, and Sony Venice. These platforms deliver identical image quality to your ground camera package, ensuring seamless integration in high-end productions where camera matching is essential. They're the choice for feature films, Super Bowl commercials, and any production where compromise isn't an option.

Payload Capacity
Up to 15kg
Camera Options
RED, ARRI, Sony
Flight Time
15-25 min
Setup Time
30-45 min

Signature Shot Types

Hero Establishing Shots

Opening sequences that demand the absolute best image quality for theatrical release.

Camera-Matched Aerials

Seamless integration with ground footage. Same sensor, same color, same quality.

Specialty Lens Work

Anamorphic, vintage glass, and specialty optics that smaller drones can't carry.

Commonly Used For

Feature Films Netflix/HBO Originals Super Bowl Commercials Theatrical Releases IMAX Productions High-End Automotive

  • Any lens combination possible
  • RAW recording at highest quality
  • Professional gimbal stabilization

‚úó Limitations

  • Highest cost platform
  • Shortest flight times
  • Longest setup time
  • Requires large crew (3-4 minimum)
  • Limited to favorable weather only
  • Requires significant landing zone

FPV Drones

Immersive, Dynamic Movement

FPV (First Person View) drones have revolutionized action cinematography. Flying through tight spaces, following cars at 150 mph, or creating impossible one-take sequences‚ÄîFPV creates shots that no other platform can achieve. From lightweight 5" builds carrying GoPros to cinema FPV rigs with RED cameras, there's an FPV solution for every production level. The immersive, dynamic movement style has become a defining visual language of modern filmmaking.
