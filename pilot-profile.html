About Marcus

Marcus Chen is an Emmy-nominated FPV cinematographer with 8 years of experience shooting high-end commercial work, feature films, and major television productions. Based in Los Angeles, he specializes in dynamic aerial sequences and fast-paced cinematography using RED Komodo cameras. His work has been featured on Netflix, HBO, and major studio productions.

Known for his innovative approach to drone cinematography and ability to operate in challenging environments, Marcus has worked with world-class directors and cinematographers on some of Hollywood's biggest productions. He brings technical expertise, creative problem-solving, and a collaborative approach to every project.
